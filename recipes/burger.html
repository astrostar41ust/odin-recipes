burger

Description

A juicy beef patty topped with melted cheese, crisp lettuce, ripe tomato, and tangy pickles, all nestled in a toasted bun

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Salt and pepper
  • Burger buns
  • Ketchup, mustard, and mayonnaise
  • Tomato slices
  • Cheese sliceslt
  • Lettuce leaves

Steps

  1. Form Patties: Shape the ground beef into patties, about 1/2 inch thick. Season with salt and pepper.
  2. Cook Patties: Heat a skillet or grill over medium-high heat. Cook the patties for about 4-5 minutes per side, or until desired doneness. Add cheese slices during the last minute to melt.
  3. Toast Buns: Spread butter on the insides of the buns and toast them on the skillet or grill until golden brown.
  4. Assemble: Place the cooked patties on the bottom buns. Top with lettuce, tomato, pickles, and onions. Add condiments like ketchup, mustard, and mayonnaise. Cover with the top buns.
  5. Serve: Enjoy your delicious homemade cheeseburger!
